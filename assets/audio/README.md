# Audio Files for Child Voice

## How to Add Real Child Voice Recordings

To make the app sound more natural and engaging, you can record a real child's voice saying the feedback phrases.

### Required Audio Files (MP3 format):

**Correct Answer Phrases:**
- `correct_1.mp3` - "Awesome! You got it right!"
- `correct_2.mp3` - "Fantastic! That's correct!"
- `correct_3.mp3` - "Excellent work!"
- `correct_4.mp3` - "You're a math star!"
- `correct_5.mp3` - "Amazing! Well done!"
- `correct_6.mp3` - "Super job!"
- `correct_7.mp3` - "Brilliant!"

**Wrong Answer Phrases:**
- `wrong_1.mp3` - "Oops! Not quite."
- `wrong_2.mp3` - "Nice try!"
- `wrong_3.mp3` - "Almost there!"
- `wrong_4.mp3` - "Good effort!"

### Recording Tips:

1. **Use a child aged 8-12** for the most relatable voice
2. **Record in a quiet room** to avoid background noise
3. **Speak clearly and enthusiastically** with natural emotion
4. **Keep recordings short** (2-3 seconds each)
5. **Use a good quality microphone** or smartphone
6. **Save as MP3 format** at 128kbps or higher

### How to Record:

1. Use a voice recording app on your phone
2. Have the child say each phrase with enthusiasm
3. Export as MP3
4. Name files exactly as listed above
5. Place all MP3 files in this `assets/audio/` folder

### Alternative: Use Text-to-Speech

If you cannot record a real child's voice, the app will use the device's built-in text-to-speech with optimized settings for a more child-friendly sound (higher pitch, natural pace).

### Free Voice Recording Tools:

- **Audacity** (Desktop) - Free audio editor
- **Voice Recorder** (Android/iOS) - Built-in apps
- **GarageBand** (iOS/Mac) - Free audio recording
- **WavePad** (Android/iOS) - Free audio editor

### Online TTS Services (Alternative):

If you want computer-generated but higher quality voices:
- **Google Cloud Text-to-Speech** - Has child/young voices
- **Amazon Polly** - Neural voices sound more natural
- **Microsoft Azure Speech** - Has child voice options

Note: These services require API keys and may have costs.
